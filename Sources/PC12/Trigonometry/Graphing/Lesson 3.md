## Transformations

Graph $y = -2 \tan 3x + 1$

* $\alpha$: 2
* Reflected
* $\delta$: $\uparrow$ 1
* $\beta$: 3
* $P$: $\frac{\pi}{3}$
* $Q_P$: $\frac{\pi}{12}$
* Domain: $\{x|x\not = \frac{\pi}{6} + \frac{\pi}{3}n, n \in \mathbb{Z}, x \in \mathbb{R}\}$
